First Blog Entry - Ideas and Whatnot

I have little to say here, except that this is the first entry in this blog, and I can only hope that I continue.

At the moment there is still much to be worked out, but with time this should come to look a lot better presentationally (although I will never do any fancy effects with Javascript, this site will stay clean and light) and I can add more entries.

I have a couple ideas for particular subdivisions I could have for different kinds of entries. Some of these are:

pie.sateoki.xyz For information about PIE - I have always found information on Proto-Indo-European to be difficult to understand, so I would like to look into it and present it clearly.
deu.sateoki.xyz For explaining German grammar and interesting quirks
jpn.sateoki.xyz Same but for Japanese grammar
progblog.sateoki.xyz A blog about programming and computer science
etym.sateoki.xyz For interesting etymologies

There will likely be more to come but for now I will work on these kinds of things, and add any more blog posts where I think of something maybe interesting to write/read about.
