Ideas for the webgarden

  • The player can move about, inspect with say, Z, and dig with X.
  • At one point, the player could dig, and find things. E.g. a compressed rat, with the message Whilst digging, your fork goes through a dead rat, that's clearly been crushed by years of tectonic pressures. You decide to replace the rat in the ground and cover him over., or, You find a colony of red ants. Ouch, they bite!, etc.
  • The player could find an apple tree and shake it to get apple-related information.
  • The player could carve their and their partners name into the tree for the next players to be able to see. Potentially also the same with a bridge and locks.
  • When inspecting flowers, they bring to mind an idea. This is the blog post. Other ways of including the posts is: there is a fallen bit of paper on the ground, which it is written on; there is a book on the floor with notes; the flower reminds you of an idea; you sit under a tree and ponder the idea; etc.
