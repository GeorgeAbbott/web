Training

A reference for the training which I have been doing. I have decided on a regular schedule (as per a blog post I saw, will link when I see it) and will try to do this everyday. Then I will update it.

I do 25x sit-ups, 30s (at least - more if possible) plank, 25 squats, 25 ball squats (I don't know what to call these. Hold a 10kg training ball; lift it up above the head, then behind the head; then bring it back, and squat with it; this should return you to a neutral position, that's one rep.)

The full workout is only about seven to ten minutes, about the time it takes to listen to, say, Veterans of the Psychic Wars. So I can listen to a sick riff whilst doing my exercise for the day.
