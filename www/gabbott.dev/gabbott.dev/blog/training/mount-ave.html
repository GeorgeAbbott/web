Mount Avenue

This is a street in Leigh-on-Sea, which is quite tall and very steep. Not sure exactly how steep, but it's a challenge for me. I want to be able to do it, not easily maybe, but consistently, and without major trouble.

Based off figures on the internet, it is 470m long, and has a total elevation of 26m (16m at the bottom, 42m at the top). So the average gradient is 5.53%.

The rules are:

  1. Always stay on a neutral gear - my bike is 21-speed so for me that is 2nd gear on the front and 4th on the rear.
  2. Always stay in the saddle.
