Songs I like

This page has a list of songs I like. That's about it.

Touhou

Touhou has far too many good songs. I'll list what I can.

  • Matsukaze Aoba: Best of Touhou Funk / Swing Jazz Songs - whole thing is worth listening, every song is good.
  • Matsukaze Aoba: Touhou Jazz Medley - see above. The whole thing is really good. The Witches Ball at 1:18:45 is incredible.

Rock

  • Kjøtt - a Norwegian band, who were only around for two years, and only released two albums. Among their best are Instamatic, Jeg vil bli som Jesus, Kloning, Se og Hør, Elektrisk, and best of all, Hue Uten Sanser.
  • Other Norwegian songs that are good are Fittesatan anarkikommando from Mannen som elsket Yngve, and Når Knoklene blir til Gele, as well as Aldri mer. I need to listen to more Raga Rockers.
  • Blue Oyster Cult - basically every song is good. This version of Astronomy has a mad riff. So does Veterans of the Psychic Wars. And the SEE version of Astronomy as well. Buck never disappoints! Apart from that, Hammer Back, Burnin for you, Nosferatu, Perfect Water, Real World, Stone of Love ... there's too many good ones.

Folk Metal

Folk metal is an amazing genre.

  • Eluveitie - Inis Mona, Slania's Song, A Rose for Epona, Helvetios, Santonian Shores among others.
  • Ensiferum - Lai Lai Hei of course, and Andromeda. Heathen Throne is good too.
