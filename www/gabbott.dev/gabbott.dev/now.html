Now

The now section of a website is an idea by Derek Sivers, in which you add a page to your website, entitled now, giving an overview on what you are up to now. Nothing too complicated. I tend, in my journalling, to write a mer - shortened down from month end review, as well as a yer for the yearly review, in addition to my daily diary keeping. I will consider moving the mers over to here from now on.

September 2023

I've started to read more, which I've not done as much as wanted to the past few months. The Acquisitive Society, Longitude, The Last Man, I've been reading, and hopefully before the end of the month The Kybalion. I've also decided to, in the vein of another blog I can't remember which, start reading Plato (finally!) - at least one book a month; I've updated the website to better keep a track of what I read. I have decided to start writing a game in D and SDL2, just for learning purposes, for fun not profit, and also have begun to write up a little on a story I have wanted to write for a while now - a basic romance between two people who work at a flower nursery. It'll probably be terrible, but let's see how it goes. I've going to get a new fountain pen in early October, and looking forwards to seeing how it writes, and how the Iroshizuku ink is. If it writes really well, I may have found myself a new interest! Apart from that, I have tended to the allotment but not got much work done on it this month. I really ought to try get things done before the days become too short. I'll be going to Hope Valley in Derbyshire with a friend of mine at the end of the month, which will be enjoyable.
